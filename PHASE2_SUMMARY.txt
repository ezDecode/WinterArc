💡 HOW TO USE
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

1. Sign in to your account
2. Navigate to /today (automatic after sign in)
3. Check off your daily targets:
   • Study: Complete 4 × 1-hour blocks
   • Reading: Read 10+ pages
   • Pushups: Complete 3 sets (50 total)
   • Meditation: Meditate 10-20 minutes
   • Water: Drink 8 × 500ml bottles (4L)
4. Add optional notes (morning/evening/general)
5. Watch your score update in real-time
6. Changes save automatically after 500ms
7. Aim for 5/5 to get the "Perfect Day" badge!
